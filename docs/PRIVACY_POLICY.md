# BuzzChat Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 24, 2026**

## Introduction

BuzzChat ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address when you subscribe to Pro or Business plans via ExtensionPay
- **Settings and Preferences**: Your chat automation settings, templates, FAQ rules, and commands
- **API Keys**: For Business tier users who enable API access

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Analytics**: Aggregated counts of messages sent (welcome, FAQ, timer, commands)
- **Feature Usage**: Which features are enabled and how often they are used
- **Error Logs**: Anonymous error data to help us improve the extension

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Chat message content
- Usernames of viewers in your stream
- Personal information of your stream viewers
- Browsing history outside of Whatnot.com

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the BuzzChat service
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Improve and optimize our extension
- Provide customer support
- Send important service updates

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- Your settings are stored in your browser's sync storage
- Settings sync across devices where you're signed into Chrome/Firefox
- Settings are NOT transmitted to our servers

### Cloud Storage (ExtensionPay)
- Payment and subscription data is processed by ExtensionPay
- We receive only: payment status, plan type, and account email
- See ExtensionPay's privacy policy: https://extensionpay.com/privacy

## Third-Party Services

BuzzChat connects to the following external services:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| ExtensionPay | Payment processing | Email, payment details |
| Whatnot.com | Chat automation | Messages you choose to send |

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only:
- With your consent
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights or safety

## Data Retention

- **Settings**: Stored locally until you uninstall the extension
- **Analytics**: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
- **Payment Info**: Retained by ExtensionPay per their policies

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored by the extension in your browser
- **Delete**: Remove all data by uninstalling the extension
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics in extension settings
- **Export**: Export your settings and templates

### How to Delete Your Data
1. Open BuzzChat settings
2. Go to Settings tab
3. Click "Reset All Data" to clear all local data
4. Uninstall the extension to remove all browser storage

## Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- API keys are hashed using SHA-256 before storage
- Sensitive data is never transmitted in plaintext
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse
- Input validation to prevent injection attacks

## Children's Privacy

BuzzChat is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the extension or by email.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

## GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union:
- Legal basis for processing: Consent and legitimate interest
- Data transfers: Settings stored locally; payment data processed in US by ExtensionPay
- You may contact us to exercise your rights under GDPR
