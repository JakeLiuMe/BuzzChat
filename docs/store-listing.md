### What BuzzChat Does

**Auto-Welcome New Viewers**
Every time someone joins your stream, BuzzChat sends a personalized welcome message. "Hey {username}! Welcome to the stream!" - Automatically. Every time.

**Answer FAQs Automatically**
Tired of answering "How much is shipping?" for the 100th time? Set up FAQ auto-replies and BuzzChat handles it instantly.

**Timed Promotional Messages**
Schedule recurring messages like "Free shipping on orders over $50!" every 5 minutes. Keep viewers engaged without lifting a finger.

**Quick Templates**
One-click sending for your most used messages. Shipping info, payment details, follow reminders - all ready to go.

**Giveaway Entry Tracking**
Track who enters your giveaways in real-time. See entry counts, unique entries, and pick winners easily.

**Analytics Dashboard**
See how many messages BuzzChat sent, your peak activity hours, and which FAQ triggers are most popular.

## Additional Details

**Single Purpose Description:**
BuzzChat automates chat messages on Whatnot live streams to help sellers engage with viewers, answer questions, and send promotional messages automatically.

**Host Permission Justification:**
BuzzChat requires access to whatnot.com to detect the chat interface and send automated messages on behalf of the seller during live streams.

**Content Scripts Justification:**
The content script is needed to:
1. Detect new viewers joining the stream
2. Read chat messages for FAQ keyword matching
3. Send automated messages to the chat input
