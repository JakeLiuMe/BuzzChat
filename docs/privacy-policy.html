BuzzChat Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

Overview

BuzzChat ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome extension that helps live sellers automate chat messages during streams. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Key Points:

  • We do NOT collect or store any personal data
  • All your settings are stored locally on your device
  • We do NOT track your browsing activity
  • We do NOT sell any data to third parties

Data We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal data. BuzzChat operates entirely on your local device and does not transmit your data to any external servers (except for subscription management through ExtensionPay).

Data Stored Locally

BuzzChat stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Settings & Preferences: Your welcome message templates, timer configurations, FAQ rules, and UI preferences (like dark mode)
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous counts of messages sent for analytics display (stored locally only)
  • Subscription Status: Your subscription tier (free/pro/business) cached locally for offline functionality

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

BuzzChat uses the following third-party service:

  • ExtensionPay: For processing subscription payments. When you subscribe to Pro or Business plans, ExtensionPay handles the payment processing. Please review ExtensionPay's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment information.

We do NOT have access to your payment card details - these are handled entirely by ExtensionPay.

Permissions Explained

BuzzChat requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage: To save your settings and preferences locally on your device
  • alarms: To reset daily message counters and run scheduled tasks
  • nativeMessaging: To enable optional MCP server integration for Business tier users who want external control (e.g., through automation tools). This is entirely optional and disabled by default.
  • Host permissions (whatnot.com): To interact with the Whatnot.com website and send automated chat messages on your behalf

How We Use Your Data

Since we don't collect any data, we don't use your data. Your settings and preferences are used solely by the extension running on your device to provide the chat automation features you've configured.

Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. We don't have access to any of your data to share in the first place.

Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. The extension code has been reviewed for security vulnerabilities and follows Chrome extension security best practices.

Children's Privacy

BuzzChat is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of BuzzChat after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your data: Uninstall the extension to remove all locally stored data
  • Export your data: Use the Settings > Export feature to download your configuration
  • Cancel subscription: Manage or cancel your subscription at any time through ExtensionPay

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or BuzzChat, please contact us:

  • GitHub Issues: Report an issue

Compliance

BuzzChat complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - by not collecting personal data
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - by not selling consumer data
